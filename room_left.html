Gradually you approach Room L, your heart pounding loudly from fear that
you feel the pulses in your head. As you approach the door within 2 feet of
distance, the door to Room L automatically dissolves. You stand a little puzzled,
but before you can make out what happened a gravitational force pulls you in and the
door reappears behind you trapping you inside. After a few seconds of darkness,
thousands of red laser lines activate and begin to slowly pierce through your skull.
You try to scream in pain, but even you can’t hear yourself. Slowly, you die from
toxic brain poisoning.
