Apparently you figure that this all is a dream and proceed to call out to
see if anything responds in the room. Quietly you utter, “Is anybody there?”
Almost instantaneously, your voice reverberates back at such a large volume
that you cover your ears from the pain. A red light emits in the room revealing
that you are in a very small empty room with blank walls. The red light starts to
gradually flash on and off. Before you have a chance to know what’s going on, the
floor disappears beneath you and you fall into the abyss.
